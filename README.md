# Concordance Labs

**The SDLC Intelligence Platform.** We measure whether engineering practices hold up as development velocity increases.

## What we build

[Concordance](https://getconcordance.com) scores engineering teams against 50 open standards across 6 SDLC phases — automatically, from live signals in your SCM and project tracker. No surveys. No self-assessments. No six-month audits.

Four lenses:

- **Framework** — 50-standard maturity assessment across Requirements, Design, Development, Testing, Release, and Operations
- **Signal** — SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance evidence generated automatically from engineering signals
- **Sentinel** — AI governance layer, activates when AI tooling is detected in your codebase
- **Bastion** — deployment risk assessment for teams shipping at high velocity

## The open standard

The [Concordance Framework](https://getconcordance.com/framework) is open — CC BY 4.0, free to adopt as your engineering SDLC standard. No procurement required.

Machine-readable at:
```
https://getconcordance.com/api/framework/standards
```

50 standards. 6 phases. `velocityGovernance` flags for the standards that degrade fastest under AI-accelerated development.

## Velocity Governance

As AI agents increase development velocity, the practices that govern software quality — review depth, ownership, decision trails, deployment controls — come under pressure. [Velocity Governance](https://velocitygovernance.com) is the discipline of keeping those practices in step with output speed.

10 Concordance standards carry elevated risk under AI acceleration. They're documented at [velocitygovernance.com](https://velocitygovernance.com) and flagged in the API.
